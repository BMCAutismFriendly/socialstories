Blood Pressure

This story is about how the nurse will measure my blood pressure. I can tell the nurse anytime if I need a break. The nurse needs to do her job so I can stay healthy.

I will sit down. The nurse will wrap a cloth around my arm. It might feel tight.

The cloth will get tighter, but that's okay. It is important that I sit still.

The cloth will stop feeling tight. The nurse will read my blood pressure from the machine.

The nurse will remove the cloth. I can ask the nurse to tell me my blood pressure.
