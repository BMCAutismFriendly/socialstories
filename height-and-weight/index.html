Height and Weight

This story is about how the nurse will measure my height and weight. I can tell the nurse anytime if I need a break. The nurse needs to do her job so I can stay healthy.

I will step onto the scale and lean my back against the height rod.

I will stand tall and still while the nurse weighs me.

I will continue to stand tall and still while the nurse measures my height.

I will step off the scale. I can ask the nurse to tell me my height and weight.
