Getting A Shot

This story is about how the nurse will give me a shot. If I feel nervous, I can

  • Think of something that makes me happy
  • Ask someone to hold my hand
  • Look away
  • Remember that I'm staying strong and healthy

I will roll up my sleeve.

The nurse will clean a spot on my arm by wiping it with a damp alcohol pad. It might feel cold. I also might smell something different.

It's time for the shot. I can choose to look away and ask someone to hold my hand.

The nurse will give me the shot. It might feel like a pinch. It is important that I sit still.

The nurse will put a Band-Aid on top of my arm.
