Physical Exam

This story is about getting a physical exam. I can tell the doctor anytime if I need a break. The doctor needs to do her job so I can stay healthy.

I may have to take off some of my clothes and put on a paper dress.

The doctor will feel different parts of my body to make sure everything is okay.

The doctor may check my eyes, ears, and throat.

The doctor may listen to my heart and ask me to take deep breaths.

The doctor might tap softly on my elbow, knee, or ankle.

After the doctor finishes checking me, I can put my clothes back on.

I did a great job learning about my physical exam!
