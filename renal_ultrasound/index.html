Getting a Renal (Belly) Ultrasound

This is a story about getting pictures of my belly.
The doctor wants to make sure my belly is healthy!

If I feel scared, I can:

  • Ask someone to hold my hand
  • Think of something that makes me happy
  • Remember that I am staying healthy and strong

    • I will take off my clothes so I can put on the special robe!

    An ultrasound technician with gloves will come into the room. They will take the pictures of my belly.
    I will lay down on the table and someone can hold my hand
    if I need it.

    The ultrasound technician will put a warm sticky jelly on my belly or back.
    They will place a magic wand on my belly or back. The wand helps them to take pictures.
    There will be a screen where I can see the pictures of my belly. How cool!

    I will remember that if I feel scared, I can:

  • Ask someone to hold my hand.
  • Think of something that makes me happy.
  • Remember that I am staying healthy and strong!

    • After the pictures are done, I can wipe the sticky jelly off my back or belly with a paper towel.
    Someone will help me with this if I need it.

    I will now take off the comfortable special robe and put on my clothes.

    I did a great job learning how to get pictures of my belly.
    I am so brave!
