Doctor's Office

This story is about how I will get to my doctor's appointment.

I need to go see my doctor so I can be healthy and strong.

I will first arrive at the building.

I will go inside and walk to the elevator.

I will take the elevator to the floor that the office is on.

I will walk to the doctor's office.

I will go to the front desk. Then I will tell the person my name and that I am here to see my doctor.

I will sit in the waiting room and wait for my name to be called. I can choose to do an activity.

When my name is called, I will follow the nurse.
