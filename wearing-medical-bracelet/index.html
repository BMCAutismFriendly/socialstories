Wearing a Medical Bracelet

This story is about learning to wear a medical bracelet.

I am getting a MedicAlert bracelet. I am going to wear this bracelet around my wrist.

I am not going to take my bracelet off. I can eat, sleep, and take a bath with my bracelet on. My bracelet stays on at all times.

It is VERY important that I keep my bracelet on. My bracelet will help keep me safe. If I ever get lost, the bracelet will help my parents find me.

If I am safe, everyone will be happy! I will be happy, too!
