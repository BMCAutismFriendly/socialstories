Dentist Appointment

This story is about going to a dentist's appointment. The dentist needs to help keep my teeth healthy and strong.

I will sit in a big chair that lays back.

The dentist will put a special bib on me to keep me clean.

The dentist will turn on a special light to see inside my mouth. It might be bright.

I will need to keep my mouth open so the dentist can look in my mouth.

The dentist will use different tools to make sure my teeth are healthy.

The dentist might clean my teeth. The toothbrush at the dentist is different than mine. It might make a noise. I might have to rinse my mouth with water.

The dentist might take pictures of my teeth.

When I finish my exam, I will get a small prize.

I did a great job learning about my dentist appointment!
